About TEDxBITNoida

TEDxBITNoida is an independently organized TED event that brings together thinkers, innovators, and change-makers to share ideas that matter. Following the TED mantra of "Ideas Worth Spreading," our event will showcase inspiring talks, performances, and discussions.

This year marks our 5th edition, and we're proud to continue this tradition of intellectual stimulation and community building at the BIT Noida campus.

About TED

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four-day conference in California 30 years ago, TED has grown to support its mission with multiple initiatives.

About BIT Noida

This Year's Theme

Beyond Boundaries: Redefining Possibilities

In a world that's constantly evolving, we find ourselves at the intersection of tradition and innovation. This year's TEDxBITNoida explores ideas that transcend conventional limits - in technology, society, art, and human potential.

Join us as we examine how breaking barriers can lead to groundbreaking discoveries and transformative experiences.
